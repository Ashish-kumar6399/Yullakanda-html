ABOUT US

About Us

Banjaarey Travels is passionate about creating unforgettable travel experiences, with a focus on organizing experience-based treks, unique stays, and travel packages both within India and internationally. Founded in 2018, we have been connecting travelers and creating cherished memories by catering to the adventure and travel aspirations of trekking enthusiasts, hikers, backpackers, and explorers. Based out of Uttarakhand, Banjaarey Travels is your one-stop solution for meeting fellow adventurers, learning from seasoned travelers, and finding exciting journeys that match every budget.

We specialize in a wide range of travel adventures including trekking through glaciers, meadows, and scenic trails, backpacking, peak climbing, wildlife explorations like Jeep and Elephant safaris in Rajaji National Park and Corbett Tiger Reserve, as well as exhilarating activities like cycling, biking, paragliding, river rafting, hot-air ballooning, and motorbike expeditions to Leh Ladakh and Spiti. We also offer pilgrimage tours such as Chardhaam and Hemkund Sahib, and international travel packages to destinations like Vietnam and Thailand.
