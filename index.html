About

Yulla Kanda

Location: Yulla Kanda is a mountain peak located in the Garhwal Himalayas, Uttarakhand, India.

Elevation: It stands at an elevation of 6,200 meters (20,341 feet) above sea level.

Trekking destination: Yulla Kanda is a popular trekking destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Scenic views: The peak offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, valleys, and glaciers.

Remote location: Yulla Kanda is situated in a remote area, requiring a challenging trek to reach the summit.

Best time to visit: The best time to visit Yulla Kanda is during the summer months (June to September).

Climbing permits: Trekkers need to obtain permits and follow local regulations to climb Yulla Kanda.

Local guides: It is recommended to hire local guides or join a guided trek to navigate the challenging terrain.
